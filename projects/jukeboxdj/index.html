A full DJ rig, down to the groove

Under the hood is a custom vinyl engine on Web Audio: the platter has motor inertia and hand friction, so everything you do to the record happens to the sound.

🤖

AI Auto-Mix

Load a track on each deck and let the AI DJ the transition: it beat-matches, phase-aligns the kicks, then blends them. Pick from 10 hands-on techniques — bass swaps, filter sweeps, echo throws, loop rolls, spinbacks, double drops — or hand the whole set to one of 10 autonomous AI DJ modes.

🖐️

True vinyl scratching

Grab the platter and move it — audio tracks your hand sample-accurately, backwards and forwards. Let go and the motor spins back up like real wax.

💿

Scratch FX pads

Ten one-tap vinyl hits — baby, chirp, transform, spinback, rewind, brake and more — synthesized live on the master bus. Fire them by pad or the number keys 1–0.

🎚️

Real mixer

Equal-power crossfader, channel faders, 3-band EQ with kills, and a sweepable low-pass/high-pass filter per deck. Throw the fader with Z X C. The whole console shifts colour and floats notes to the beat.

🎵

Beat-matching

±8% pitch faders with live BPM readouts, plus one-tap SYNC that pulls a deck to its partner's tempo — learn to beat-match, or cheat gracefully.

🔁

Loops & echo

Beat-synced 1/2/4/8 loops that lock to the record's BPM, and a tempo-synced echo send per channel for build-ups and drops.

📀

The Jukebox

Six original tracks — house, boom bap, trap, techno, breaks, synthwave — pressed on-device the moment you open the app. Then drop in your own MP3s; BPM is auto-detected.

⏺️

Record your set

One tap records the master bus — scratches, loops, echoes and all — and hands you the file. Nothing is uploaded anywhere, ever.
