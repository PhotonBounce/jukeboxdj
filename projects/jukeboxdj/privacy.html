JukeboxDJ — Privacy Policy

Effective: July 2026

The short version

JukeboxDJ runs entirely in your browser. It has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no server that receives your data.

Your music

Audio files you add to the Jukebox are decoded and played locally on your device. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere outside your browser session. Closing the tab discards them.

Recordings

Mix recordings are created on your device and saved only where you choose to save them. We never see or receive them.

Data we collect

None. The app uses a service worker to cache its own files on your device so it can load fast and work offline; that cache contains only the app itself.

Contact

Questions? Reach us via photon-bounce.com.
